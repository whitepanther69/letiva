Avitel Mission

At Avitel, we specialize in design, engineering, programming, installing, and maintaining smart home technology and commercial automation systems. Our expertise lies in providing seamless control of integrated technology and automation systems, encompassing audio/video, lighting, motorized shades, climate control, energy and power management, networking, and surveillance/security systems. With a customer-oriented approach and a commitment to providing high-quality service, we ensure that our clients will benefit from advanced technology tailored to their specific needs, whether in your home or business area.

Since our inception in 1998, our mission has remained steadfast: to provide individuals and businesses with innovative technology solutions that simplify and enrich their lives. We strive to foster enduring relationships with our customers by consistently surpassing their expectations and earning their trust through exceptional performance.

For over two decades, we have served the greater Phoenix area and numerous out-of-state projects. However, our greatest source of pride lies in the strong partnerships we have made with our trade partners and loyal customers, many of whom have been with us since the beginning. As a certified dealer for Crestron, Lutron, Savant, and Control4 home automation control systems, we remain committed to providing cutting-edge solutions that enhance your lifestyle or business operations.

We extend a warm invitation to you to experience the Avitel difference and become part of our family. Whether you're seeking assistance with your technology needs or exploring ways to elevate your living or working environment, we're here to help. Welcome to Avitel, where innovation meets unparalleled service.
